About Badminton

Badminton is a fast-paced racquet sport played indoors or outdoors, either singles or doubles. Rallies are quick, the shuttlecock is lightweight, and matches reward agility, strategy, and timing.

Why people love it

  • Easy to start: a racquet, shuttle, and a bit of space.
  • Great workout: cardio, reflexes, and coordination.
  • Inclusive: fun for all ages and skill levels.
  • Convenient: most communities have indoor courts.

Basic rules at a glance

  1. Best of three games to 21 points (win by 2).
  2. Rally scoring: every rally earns a point.
  3. Serve diagonally; switch ends at 11 points in a game.
