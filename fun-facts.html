Fun Facts About Badminton

Discover interesting and surprising facts about your favorite sport!

1. Fastest Racket Sport

The shuttlecock can travel over 300 km/h in professional games!

2. Olympic Sport

Badminton has been an Olympic sport since 1992.

3. Origin of the Name

Named after Badminton House in England.

4. Lightweight Shuttlecock

Weighs just 4.74–5.50 grams—super light and fast!

5. International Popularity

Huge in Asia—especially China, Indonesia, and India.

6. Record-Breaking Match

The longest match lasted 124 minutes in the 2016 Olympics!
